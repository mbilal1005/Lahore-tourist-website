7 top Cinemas in Lahore

Lahore is famous for its rich culture and heritage. Ever since the independence of

Pakistan, it has been the centre of Pakistani cinema and many phenomenal actors have started

their careers in this city. As home to Pakistan’s entertainment industry, it is obvious that Lahore is

filled with people who share a deep love for watching films on the big screen. With this love,

Lahore has a huge number of cinemas both in old and modern styles that cater to the masses with

a wonderful cinema experience. The film industry of Pakistan has been thriving and the people of

Lahore love to celebrate this revival of the film industry in Cinemas. In recent years, many new

cinemas in modern style have been built to match the enthusiasm of film lovers in Lahore.

Top Cinemas in Lahore





Cue Cinema, Main Boulevard Gulberg

Cue cinema has excellent visuals and incredible sound quality with the latest audio equipment. This cinema also offers exclusive services for VIP customers The cinema also has an RGB laser projector which provides the highest quality movie watching experience with its bright and powerful features. Some of the snacks that Cue cinema offers are popcorn, fries, burgers, and beverages.

Cinepax, Packages Mall – Fortress Mall – Lake City – Amanah Mall

Cinepax cinemas are one of the finest cinemas in Pakistan. Operating in 9 cities around Pakistan, they are one of the largest cinema networks in Pakistan. They offer 2D and 3D experience along with the best sound equipment. To offer an international quality cinema experience, the cinema has three different halls, platinum, gold, and silver. The gold halls have reclining armchairs along with popcorn, candies, coffees, and burgers. Platinum halls are even more luxurious as they offer footrests and private tables. The best part about platinum halls is that you can order your snacks as you please throughout the movie. Cinepax also has arranged a VIP experience with the CMAXX screen and offers recliners with armrests, a side table, along with the biggest cinema screen.

Universal Cinemas, Emporium Mall

Universal cinemas offer a rich cinema experience by offering three screens, Ultra, Gold, and Premium. The Ultra cinema halls have huge screens with art technology, excellent quality sounds, and Dolby Atmos technology. The gold halls have comfortable seating that will allow you to feel at home and for those of you who want an intimate experience then exclusive gold halls are perfect for you. The Premium halls will provide you with luxurious seating and the state of art premium chairs are equipped with the latest glider technology to enhance your experience. What’s more, is that UNIVERSAL CINEMAS offers private suites for corporate meetings or private events. Some of the food options in the cinema are popcorn, burgers, nachos, fries, and beverages. You’ll also find a cafe, car parking, free wifi, and wheelchairs for the elderly and disabled.

Cinestar, Township Lahore – Xinhua Mall – DHA Raya Phase 6

If you want to take your family on a movie trip, Cinestar will offer you a high-quality family-friendly experience. It is one of the first IMAX cinemas in Pakistan. You’ll also find food counters offering the best popcorn and a huge variety of snacks. The cinema also offers a very comfortable seating arrangement and you can always make a reservation for the ticket online.

Super Cinema, Royal Palm Canal Road and Vogue Tower

Super Cinema Royal Palm has high-quality sound processors and state of art experience. With comfortable seating and the best technical equipment, you will certainly love watching a movie at this cinema if you are looking for a luxurious movie-watching experience. You can also enjoy popcorn, snacks, and beverages throughout the movie.
Super cinema has everything you need in a cinema. From luxurious and comfortable seating to features of digital cinema, it offers it all. With a modern design, Super cinema is certainly a good place for those who want to have a comfortable movie watching experience.

The Arena, Bahria Orchard

The Arena cinema is one of the best cinemas in Lahore. They have seating arrangements with state of art. Along with 2D and 3D experiences, you will also find the Arena to have high-quality sound equipment. Whether you want to enjoy popcorn and plain tea or you want some nachos with Pepsi, the cinema has a huge variety of food options and beverages.

Raiha CineGold Plex, Bahria Town

Raiha CineGold Plex is a multiplex cinema. With its dine-in movie theatre experience, you will be entertained by food served at your place. You can choose and order any dish from the variety of delicious continental cuisine they offer. They also have three luxury lounges with fully reclining seats and the best part is cutting edge sound technology. If you want an exclusive luxurious movie-watching experience then Raiha CineGold Plex would be your best bet.



If you are someone who loves watching movies and happens to visit the City of Gardens then these top cinemas of Lahore will certainly be a great choice especially because the experience, they offer is incredible. Not only do they have a family-friendly environment that caters to the modest families of Pakistan, but they are also super comfortable and are equipped with the best audio and screen technologies such as Dolby Atmos so that your movie watching experience will be enhanced by 100%.
